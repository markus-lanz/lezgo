MARKETS
DECORATIVE COATINGS

The new cluster Decorative Coatings reflects the diversity of the sector. It supplies additives for half of the global paint and coating production and supports customers in all regions in which BYK is represented. The cluster includes additives for

  • Architectural coatings (including roof coatings, road markings, woodcare products)
  • Flooring coatings
  • Construction (construction chemicals)

Key products:

Architectural: DISPERBYK-199, BYK-1640, LAPONITE S-482, the OPTIFLO family

Flooring: DISPERBYK-2152 and BYK-1794

Construction: BYK-1690 SD, BYK-1691 SD, the OPTIBENT family
