WOOD & FURNITURE

Depending on the application, industrial wood coatings have to satisfy different demands. BYK additives can be found in coatings for

  • Furniture and living accessories
  • Flooring, windows, and doors
  • Instruments, boats or toys
  • Wood panels (solid, and also MDF)

Key products:

DISPERBYK-2008, -2009, -2015 and -2155; BYK-349, -378, -3455 and the BYK-UV 35 family; BYK-028, BYK-093, BYK-1798; AQUAMAT 272, CERAFLOUR 929 and CERAFLOUR 1000; BYK-410, BYK-420 and the OPTIFLO family
