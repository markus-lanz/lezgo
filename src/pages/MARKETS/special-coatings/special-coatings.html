In the Special Coatings Liquid division our additives optimize

  • packaging coatings, so-called can coatings, for example for beverage cans, aluminum capsules or metal closures,
  • metal applications, so-called coil coatings, in which the sheet metal is first coated and then shaped; applications include fa√ßades, refrigerators or roofs,
  • industrial coatings for mobile phones and toys, tractors, cranes, and machinery.

In the Special Coatings Powder division, the applications range from

  • bicycles and vehicle parts to
  • garden furniture and domestic appliances.

Their special features: they are solvent-free and powdered. All raw materials are mixed, ground and then sprayed and baked. This fuses the powder into a resistant coating. Thomas Czeczatka continues to hold global responsibility for powder coating products.
