PRODUCT GROUPS
Wetting and dispersing additives

Lead to fine-sized solid particles and their uniform distribution in liquid media and this way ensure the long-term stability of the systems.

Surface additives

Improve, resp. prevent surface defects, optimize surface-slip or achieve an optimized cleanability.

Rheology additives

Are used to influence the flow-behavior to improve e.g. properties like processability and the application behavior.

Defoamers and air release additives

Prevent and destroy foam bubbles and this way lead to an improved processing, a perfect surface and optimum product properties.

Wax additives

Regulate for example the processability of products or improve the surface properties.

Adhesion promoters and Coupling Agents

Create stable and as many as possible additional bonds between solids and liquids, or different solid structures.

Viscosity reducers

Regulate the the viscosity in e. g. PVC-plastisols and this way ensure process safety and constant product quality.

Processing additives

Processing additives are used in a number of plastics applications. These have generally positive influences on a wide range of processing parameters and the properties of the final product.
